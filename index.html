Welcome to South Korea

Nestled on the Korean Peninsula between mountains and sea, South Korea seamlessly blends over five millennia of dynastic heritage-seen in its palaces, temples, and folk villages-with cutting-edge urban energy in Seoul, Busan, and beyond. From the volcanic landscapes of Jeju Island to the culinary delights of kimchi and bibimbap, and the global influence of K-Pop and cinema, Korea invites you to explore its traditions, innovations, and warm hospitality.
